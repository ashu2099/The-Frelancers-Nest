About MakeMyNotes


This is a firm which serves eBook products to students. Basically, our firm believes in digitization and for education, it is a must. So our mission is to create the best virtual publication for our customers where they can purchase eBooks as well as digital book products - we provide eBooks for customers and also, physical printed books for sale and also give a platform for our consumer streams to grow.

We have books about three major streams:
1) Bachelor of commerce. (B.Com)
2) Bachelor of Science. (Bsc)
3) Bachelor of science in computer science. (Bsc-CS)
4) Bachelor of management studies. (BMS)
