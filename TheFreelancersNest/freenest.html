Hello

Welcome to The Freelancer's Nest. The home of all the free birds who like to have their own independent businesses and jobs. We serve as a common platform for freelancers as well as individual and multinational business organizations to find each other and recruit workers or apply and bid for jobs. These jobs are based on a one-time service or continuous support regarding a project and building up a good clientele for future collaborations or referrals. So if you're looking for good talent to cater to your services or to find a job that suits your needs, you know you have come to the right place.

For Freelancers

A freelancer is a self-employed person. Freelancing is so flexible, you can set your own hours, working full or part-time on the projects of your choice. While clients can offer specifications to the work, a freelancer works similar to an independent contractor, in which he's free to control how the work is completed. Freelancing allows you to set your own price for a job. Many freelancers work for the same set of clients over a long period of time. Others work with clients over shorter periods, usually on specific projects.

Getting started as a freelancer is as easy. As soon as you register, you can set up a profile,upload portfolios, find work, and networking with your current sphere of influence to find your first client. Here's steps to building a freelance career:

 Decide what you'll offer.
 Determine your target market.
 Create an online portfolio.
 Set your prices.
 Start reaching out to find clients and incresing your experience for further progress and referrals.

For Companies

The type of work freelancers do varies. Nearly every type of service a business would need could be provided by a freelancer. Marketing, Copywriting, Publicity, Writing, Technological Support, Web programming, Web design, Mobile App development, Software development, Creative works such as Graphic design, Photography, Financial services such as bookkeeping and Accounting, and much more.

Finding and recruiting freelancers for different types of jobs is also very convenient. Companies can register and login with their own accounts and start posting jobs, and get bids and invoices for jobs right away. In a nutshell, the process can be understood as follows:

 Post a job Explainging your requirements and set up an initial bid.
 Get applications regarding your job by various freelancers with different bids and experience.
 Shortlist freelancers and get in contact with them.
 Discuss constraints and deadlines for the job.
 Get the work completed and provide feedback.

About Us

The FreeLancer's Nest is a website that was essentially developed as a third year project by Ashutosh Dwivedi for the subject of Advanced Internet Technology (AIT) at Ramrao Adik Institute of Technology, Nerul, Navi Mumbai. The website features a responsive web-design developed using the Open-source technology of Bootstrap along with implementations of CSS3. The website is developed using the "Mobile-First" Approach which dictates that the website should be able to adjust to the viewport of smartphones without extra Server-side Scripting or redirection. Along with this, The website has been embedded with the Google Maps API to enable Geo-location within the website.
